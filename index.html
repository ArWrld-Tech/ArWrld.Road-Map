ArWrld Road Map

Product + Tech Jan 2018 - Future

This page serves as a high level outline of the road map for ArWrld. This is will be an ever living page, open to changes and updates. All historical aspects will be maintained via the dedicated repo on the ArWrld-Tech Github page. You will be able to explore deeper details and discussions on the ArWrld Wiki also found on our Github.

ArWrld Launch

Time will be spent harmonizing resources, spreading awareness, releasing and open sourcing tech demos, providing documentation, and finalizing structure.

Feb 2018

ArWrld Explorer Android App Release

Our ArWrld Explorer app will be launched on Android. This will feature a technology showcase of Augmented Reality including ARCore, Mapping/location, geo-data, and others related to the forth coming full platform. The Explorer app will also feature the ability to explore price estimates for the ArWrld Augmented Reality environment.

Feb 6th 2018

Pre-Sale

ArWrld will offer a token pre-sale. Check our main site to see details on how to join. At the current time, this pre-sale will account for up to 7% of the total supply. Please reference our White Paper for a more detailed distribution breakdown.

Opens March 16th

ArWrld Explorer iOS App Release

Our ArWrld Explorer app will be launched on iOS. This will feature a technology showcase of Augmented Reality including ARKit, Mapping/location, geo-data, and others related to the forth coming full platform. The Explorer app will also feature the ability to explore price estimates for the ArWrld Augmented Reality environment.

Mid March 2018

Token Sale

ArWrld will offer a Token Sale. At the current time, this Token Sale will account for up to 39% of the total supply. Please reference our White Paper for a more detailed distribution breakdown.

April 23rd - June 7th

Phase 1 Property Sale

ArWrld Phase 1 property sale will begin. This will allow for purchasing of properties with certain limitations and lowered starting prices for initial adopters. These properties will be predefined locations and property plots with an open auction system. The properties will be published prior to auctions opening, with the ArWrld community being able to submit further properties to be included. Please check our white paper for exact details.

June 2018

iOS Beta

ArWrld will launch in beta phase on ARKit supported iOS devices. This will allow for initial interactions with properties in the ArWrld. Only verified ArWrld token holders will have access to beta programs.

July 2018

Android Beta

ArWrld will launch in beta phase on ARCore supported Android devices. This will allow for initial interactions with properties in the ArWrld. Only verified ArWrld token holders will have access to beta programs.

July 2018

ArWrld Marketplace Beta

The ArWrld Marketplace beta will be launched. This will open up possibilities for buying, selling, and renting properties and 3D models/interactive elements. Please check our white paper for exact details.

Late July 2018

Phase 2 Property Sale

ArWrld Phase 2 property sale will begin. This will allow for purchasing of properties with further removed limitations, along with increased starting prices. Please check our white paper for exact details.

August 2018

Android and iOS Public Release

ArWrld v1.0 Android and iOS apps will be fully publicly released.

October 2018

Open Property Sales

Properties of all kinds will be open for purchase at this time. This will allow for purchasing of properties with further removed limitations, along with increased starting prices. Please check our white paper for exact details.

October 2018

Research

Research will continue throughout 2018. This includes tech improvements, new features, assessing and implementing community request, and expansions to new Augmented Reality platforms. As previously mentioned, this page will continue to change and update as our road map expands.
