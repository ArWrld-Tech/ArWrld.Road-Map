ArWrld Road Map

Product + Tech Jan 2018 - Future

This page serves as a high level outline of the road map for ArWrld. This is will be an ever living page, open to changes and updates. All historical aspects will be maintained via the dedicated repo on the ArWrld-Tech Github page. You will be able to explore deeper details and discussions on the ArWrld Wiki also found on our Github.

ArWrld Launch

Time will be spent harmonizing resources, spreading awareness, releasing and open sourcing tech demos, providing documentation, and finalizing structure.

Early Feb 2018

Pre-Sale

ArWrld will offer a token pre-sale. At the current time, this pre-sale will account for up to 7% of the total supply. Please reference our White Paper for a more detailed distribution breakdown.

Late Feb 2018

ICO

ArWrld will offer an ICO. At the current time, this ICO will account for up to 27% of the total supply. Please reference our White Paper for a more detailed distribution breakdown.

March 2018

Phase 1 Property Purchase

ArWrld Phase 1 property sale will begin. This will allow for purchasing of properties backed via existing parcel backed data. Our White Paper outlines what property types will be exlucded.

April 2018

iOS Beta

ArWrld will launch in beta phase on ARKit supported iOS devices. This will allow for initial interactions with properties in the ArWrld.

May 2018

Android Beta

ArWrld will launch in beta phase on ARCore supported Android devices. This will allow for initial interactions with properties in the ArWrld.

May 2018

Phase 2 Property Purchase

ArWrld Phase 2 property sale will begin. This will allow for purchasing of properties by dropping location pins with adjustable coverage. Our White Paper outlines what property types will be exlucded and other restrictions.

June 2018

Android and iOS Public Release

ArWrld Android and iOS apps will be fully publicly released.

July 2018

Research

Research will begin and continue throughout 2018. This includes tech improvements, new features, assessing and implementing community request, and expansions to new Augmented Reality platforms. As previously mentioned, this page will continue to change and update as our road map expands.
